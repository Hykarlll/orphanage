About Us

Yayasan Wardatul Jannah Kota Damansara

Our Story

Yayasan Anak-Anak Yatim Wardatul Jannah was founded on 19 June 2009 and officially registered on 20 January 2010 under the Trustee Incorporation Act 1952. We provide shelter, care, and education for orphans, the poor, converts, and underprivileged children in Kota Damansara, Selangor.

Our aim is to nurture children's spiritual, intellectual, physical, and emotional development through Islamic teachings, academic support, and life skills training. Supported by community donations and dedicated volunteers, we continue to grow our programs and facilities for the benefit of these children.

Our mission is to help every child become a responsible, knowledgeable, and independent individual who contributes positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and loving home that offers Islamic education, academic knowledge, and practical life skills for orphans and underprivileged children.

Our Vision

To develop a generation that is spiritually, intellectually, and emotionally balanced, ready to contribute to religion, community, and nation.
